Worth knowing

Browser GPS can be overridden by dev tools or spoofing apps — this isn't a cryptographic guarantee. Its real strength is combined with face recognition: an attacker needs to fake both the location and the face at once. "Flag" mode is worth using while you dial in your radius, since indoor GPS can drift 50–100m even for someone standing on campus.
